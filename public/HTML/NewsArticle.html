Whitehead residents get cash for recycling plastics

A recycling trial is taking place in Whitehead, County Antrim. Resident are paid 20p each for every plactic found in their recycling bin. To take part in this trial, residents have to download the recycling app in thier mobile phone and start scanning.

Benefits of recycling

When you recycle your plastics and other items, Have you stop and think where do your recycling go? The plastic we recycled are used by a Lurgan-based company called Cherry pipes to make pipes. Their pipes are made from shampoo, washing-up liquid bottles and milk cartons from households. This is adding £110m to the local economy.
